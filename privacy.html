Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Fromze. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our habit tracking application.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Fromze is designed with privacy at its core. We believe that your personal data should remain exactly thatâ€”personal. That's why our application operates entirely offline, ensuring that your habit data never leaves your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Fromze operates as a completely offline application, which means:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal information is transmitted to our servers
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected
  • No third-party services are integrated for data collection

Data Storage

All your habit data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Local Storage: Your habit information, progress, and settings are stored directly on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't use cloud services to store your data
  • No External Databases: Your information never reaches our servers

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to share. Your habit information remains completely private and under your control.

Third-Party Services

Fromze does not integrate with any third-party services that could access your data. The application is self-contained and operates independently.

Data Security

While we don't collect or transmit your data, we understand the importance of data security. Your local data is protected by:

  • Your device's built-in security measures
  • No network transmission of sensitive information
  • Local encryption where applicable

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: All your data is directly accessible on your device
  • Modification: You can modify or delete your data at any time through the app
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all associated data
  • Portability: Your data stays with you on your device

Children's Privacy

Fromze does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since our app doesn't collect any data, this is inherently protected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@fromze.com

Address: Paris, France

Your Trust Matters

We built Fromze with the belief that privacy should be the default, not an afterthought. Your trust is important to us, and we're committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy protection.

Thank you for choosing Fromze for your habit tracking journey.
